Ashby One Dashboard

Quick start guide for leaders and recruiting teams

3‑step setup · Takes ~10 minutes

1) Connect your data

Link ATS and HRIS sources so dashboards stay live. Use service accounts for stable credentials.

  • Open Settings → Integrations → Add Source.
  • Grant read-only scopes; verify the sync preview.
  • Schedule nightly refreshes; enable alerts on failures.

2) Configure the dashboard

Pin the metrics you review weekly and set owners. Use tags to group boards by team.

  • Create a board → “Talent Leadership”.
  • Add widgets: Pipeline health, Time-to-fill, DEI funnel, Offer acceptance.
  • Share with leadership; lock layout once finalized.

3) Notify the right people

Keep everyone aligned with scheduled sends and threshold alerts.

  • Enable Monday 8 AM email digests for the board.
  • Set alerts: “Offer acceptance < 70%” and “Aging reqs > 30 days”.
  • Post to Slack: #talent-leadership when alerts fire.
Need help? Reach out to the Ashby One team anytime.
